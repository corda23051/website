About

The Municipality of Tukuran, located in the province of Zamboanga del Sur, is a thriving coastal town known for its abundant natural resources and vibrant agricultural economy. Blessed with rich fishing grounds and fertile lands, Tukuran has long been recognized for its bountiful marine harvest and flourishing rice production. The presence of rice mills throughout the municipality further supports the livelihood of local farmers, making agriculture one of the primary sources of income for many Tukuranons. Fishing, farming, and rice milling not only sustain the community’s daily needs but also contribute significantly to the town’s economic growth. With its strategic location and hardworking people, Tukuran continues to develop while preserving the traditions that have long sustained its identity as a hub of rich agricultural and coastal resources.

Banana

New banana varieties are constantly being developed with improved disease resistance, higher yields, and better taste and texture. This is critical given the susceptibility of banana crops to diseases like Panama disease. Sustainable farming practices are also being implemented to reduce the environmental impact of banana production. This includes water conservation techniques, integrated pest management, and minimizing the use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides. Tissue culture allows for the rapid propagation of superior banana plants, ensuring consistent quality and disease-free planting material.

Innovative Fertilizers

Nanofertilizers

Our nanofertilizers are engineered to deliver nutrients directly to plant roots, increasing their absorption and reducing the need for large applications. This is a more environmentally friendly approach to fertilization.

Biofertilizers

Our biofertilizers are made from microorganisms that help improve soil health and plant growth. This is a natural and sustainable approach to fertilization.

Smart Fertilizer

Our smart fertilizer system uses sensors to monitor soil conditions and automatically releases the right amount of nutrients at the right time. This helps ensure that plants get the exact amount of fertilizer they need, maximizing yield and minimizing waste.
